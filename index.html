What I do

I use nearly 20 years of experience in learning (and still learning) web design, to help people and businesses become more connnected.

Working a lot with small retail businesses such as Mains Electrical, I use my retail knowledge and experience along side my web design skills.

A website is a tool to engage with your customers/users and generate revenue.

Where I started

After studying Advanced GNVQ Art & design at Harlow College in my late teens, I went on to Newcastle College and completed a HND Multimedia design, where I started exploring website design.

From there I have been learning the latest practices in web coding and producing web sites for mainly retail companies that I have worked for in other capacities
